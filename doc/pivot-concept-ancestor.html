The construct a full concept lineage, the ancestors and descendants are harmonized. Harmonization is achieved by:
1. Renaming the ancestor_ and descendant_ concepts to relative_ while maintaining the original identity in a separate column
1. Arranging the ancestors in descending order by levels of separation and the descendants in ascending order
1. Combining the two datasets to create the complete lineage from the topmost ancestor to the bottommost descendant
